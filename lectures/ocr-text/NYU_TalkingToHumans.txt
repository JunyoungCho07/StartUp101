=== SLIDE 15 ===
TITLE: How to ensure an effective interview?  (J

@ Beware of confirmation bias

@ Do it in person, one at atime

@ Get subjects to tell a story

@ Look for solution hacks

@ Understand their priority

@ Follow your nose & drill down

@ Listen and STFU!

@ Have someone take notes

=== SLIDE 16 ===
TITLE: Pro Tips: Students/teams         (J

@ Practice, practice, practice

@ Don’t start with your dream customer

@ Flatter subjects

@ Be transparent

@ Follow up/stay in touch

@ Write up key insights right away

 NYU | Entrepreneurial                    16                   vy @NYUEntrepreneur

=== SLIDE 17 ===
TITLE: Pro Tips: Instructors/educators ¢/y

@ Do exercises & practice before hand
@ Hold teams accountable to >100 interviews
@ Get the class/cohort to help one another
@ Make them publish interview summaries
@ Read & critique their summaries
@ Keep them focused on the problem, not
the solution
